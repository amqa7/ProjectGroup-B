Welcome to Computational Thinking

This learning resource supports the COMP11012 unit, helping you develop essential problem-solving skills that form the foundation of computer science.

Computational thinking involves:

  • Breaking down complex problems into manageable parts (decomposition)
  • Recognizing patterns and similarities between problems
  • Abstracting away unnecessary details to focus on key elements
  • Designing and analyzing efficient algorithms
  • Understanding how data is organized and processed
