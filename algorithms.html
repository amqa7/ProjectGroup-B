What is an Algorithm?

An algorithm is a set of instructions to solve a problem, like a recipe for cooking.

Example: Making a sandwich:

  1. Take two slices of bread
  2. Spread butter on one side
  3. Add your favorite filling
  4. Put the slices together

Searching Algorithms

Linear Search

Check each item one by one until you find what you're looking for.

Like: Finding a name in an unsorted phonebook

Binary Search

Keep dividing the search area in half to find items faster.

Like: Finding a word in a dictionary

Sorting Algorithms

Bubble Sort

Compare neighbors and swap if they're in the wrong order.

Like: Sorting playing cards in your hand

Quick Sort

Pick a "pivot" and divide items into smaller and larger groups.

Like: Sorting books on a shelf by size
